Personal Anecdote

In my first semester of college, I struggled to transition to the rigorous academic responsibilities. I was often overwhelmed and it showed in my first midterm grades. I started getting tutoring, asking my professors questions at the end of lectures, and prioritizing my mental health. After finding these strategies, I was able to improve my grades by the end of the semester. I have since then included these practices into my daily schedule to ensure a balanced and successful academic experience.

Move your body

The best way to give your brain the refresh it needs after a long day of classes is to move your body.
